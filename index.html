Andre Johnson (French: Αndrέ), Andre Johnson; 29 March 1977 – Present), [1] commonly called Dre or Mr. Johnson by his peers, is a Jamaican-American social magnate, who was employed by one of the largest financial institutions in North America and is one of the world's most sought after software engineers and most famous men.
